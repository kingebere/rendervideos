📖Usage Guide
1 · Upload
Videos tab → drop MP4/MOV/WebM or switch to Screenshots to drop images/folder.
2 · Extract
Select a video → ▶ Extract. Frames appear live. Adjust FPS & dedup in options.
3 · Select & Tag
Click frames to select (Shift+click range). Then click a flow name in the right panel to assign.
4 · Edit Frames
Hover a card → ✏️ Redact boxes · 🔤 Hide words · 👤 Hide faces. Double-click = full preview.
5 · Export
Set App Name → ⬇ Export ZIP. Frames organized by flow in folders. Or 🎬 for video export.
